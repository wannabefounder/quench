# Code of Conduct

Quench follows the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community. Be respectful, assume good intent,
discuss evidence rather than people, and make room for different backgrounds and levels of
experience. Maintainers may remove content or participation that violates these expectations.

Until a dedicated conduct address is published, report conduct concerns privately through a
GitHub Security Advisory. Reports will be reviewed discreetly and as promptly as possible.
